Cross-Border Collaboration

Zinago Law Practice is a Nigerian legal and HR advisory practice. Where client matters involve foreign law or cross-border considerations, the firm works in collaboration with qualified legal practitioners in relevant jurisdictions to support clients through appropriate professional arrangements.

Foreign law advice, where required, is provided by such collaborating practitioners in their respective jurisdictions. Zinago Law Practice remains responsible for Nigerian law advisory and coordination, subject to agreed engagement terms.
