Notarial Services

Zinago Law Practice provides professional notarial services for individuals, businesses and organisations requiring legally recognised documents for use in Nigeria or abroad. Our notarial services ensure that documents are properly authenticated, certified, and legally valid for official and cross-border purposes.

We assist clients in preparing and notarising documents required for international transactions, immigration processes, corporate documentation and legal declarations.

Insight Header: Insight note

Services provided

  • Notarisation of affidavits and statutory declarations
  • Certification of true copies of documents
  • Authentication of legal and corporate documents
  • Notarisation of powers of attorney and agreements
  • Verification and witnessing of signatures
  • Notarial support for international and cross-border transactions

Cross-Border Collaboration

Zinago Law Practice is a Nigerian legal and HR advisory firm. Where client matters involve foreign jurisdictions or cross-border legal considerations, the firm collaborates with qualified legal practitioners in the relevant jurisdictions to ensure clients receive appropriate local law support.

Where advice on foreign law is required, such advice is provided by the collaborating practitioners in their respective jurisdictions. Zinago Law Practice remains responsible for Nigerian law advice and overall coordination of the legal engagement in accordance with the agreed terms of engagement.

Services provided
